Hamburger

Description

The best burger recipe for summertime grilling! This juicy burger is jam-packed with all kinds
of stuff and has no tasteless bread crumbs. Serve on buns with your favorite condiments.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Onion
  • Cheese
  • Egg
  • sauces

Steps

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  2. Meanwhile, combine ground beef, onion, cheese, egg, onion soup mix.
  3. Cook patties on the preheated grill until no longer pink in the center
